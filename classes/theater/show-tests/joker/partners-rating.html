How rating is calculated?

The formula is: (SUM OF ALL DEPOSITS in last 90 days) minus (SUM OF ALL REFUNDS in last 90 days) = RATING

So if you have deposited $10,000 USD and refunded items for $3,000 USD then your rating is: 10,000 – 3,000 = 7,000 = 7k

What does it mean for you?

Partner’s rating a kind of measure of your work efficiency and honesty. If you are a good partner – if you buy a lot and refund a little – then you know your bins and you do not waste our stuff for tests or experiments. You are also honest because you not asking for refunds of each and every dump you buy from us.

If this is the case then new bases will become available for your purchase earlier than for others thanks to your high rating. It gives you ability to see and buy new updates before other people can do that, as well as some other privileges like prioritized support.
