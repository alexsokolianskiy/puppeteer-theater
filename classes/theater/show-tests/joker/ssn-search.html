Single Search

  • Search is free of charge.
  • Results have redacted SSN and partial DOB, phone and email.
  • To see one full result (with SSN, etc.), you pay a fee ($5.00).
  • You must enter at least first and last names and at least one of the other filters (state, city and/or other).

Filters

  • Name(s) – space-separated first/middle/last names in any order.
  • DOB – year, year/month or year/month/day in this format: YYYY-MM-DD. Example: 1984 (anywhere from 1984-01-01 to 1984-12-31), 1984-10 (October 1984), 1984-10-17 (exact date).
  • State – two-letter code: NY, UT, etc.
  • City – full name of a city.
  • ZIP – first 5 digits of a postal code.
